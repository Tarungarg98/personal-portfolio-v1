Improving Information Extraction from health-related text using knowledge graphs


What is it about?


The medical domain contains massive dataset regarding the symptoms, diseases and medication. Dataset being vast, it's beyond the human capacity to analyse the past records. We aim to design an approach to improve the information extraction from the medical dataset to have a better understanding of the symptoms and the possible diseases in the present time.

Knowledge graphs are widely used in the domain of NLP to preserve the relationship present in the text. We will be designing the knowledge graph for this task and will use it to extract information from the dataset.

Team


The project is being worked as my research internship under the guidance of Prof Amit Sheth and his PhD and Post-Doc students.
